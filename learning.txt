await means "wait for this to finish before moving on" — it's needed because talking to Discord over the internet takes time.

https://discordpy.readthedocs.io/en/stable/ext/commands/cogs.html

In main.py — you use @client.tree.command because you have direct access to the client object

In a cog — you use @app_commands.command because you're inside a class and don't have access to client directly, so discord.py handles the registration for you when you load the cog
